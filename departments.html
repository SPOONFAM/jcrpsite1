Server Departments

JCRP offers a structured, realistic roleplay environment through multiple specialized departments. Each tier represents increased responsibility, training, and authority.

Tier 4 — Elite Operations

Tier 3 — State & Fire Services

State-level law enforcement handling highway patrol, advanced pursuits, investigations, and statewide operations.

County-wide policing focused on patrol, warrants, investigations, transport, and corrections.

Tier 1 — Entry Level

Probationary Civilian

Entry-level civilian roleplay. Learn server rules, roleplay standards, and progression systems before advancing.

Server Rules

Please read the following rules carefully. Breaking any of these may result in warnings, kicks, or permanent bans. Respect the community and enjoy roleplay responsibly!

1. Respect Everyone

Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks are strictly prohibited. Respect staff decisions.

2. No Meta-Gaming

Do not use out-of-character knowledge to influence in-character actions. Meta-gaming breaks immersion and is not allowed.

3. No Power-Gaming

Do not force actions on other players or control their characters without consent. Always ensure realistic roleplay interactions.

4. No Exploiting or Cheating

Any exploits, hacks, or bugs that give you an unfair advantage are strictly forbidden. Report bugs to staff immediately.

5. Appropriate Roleplay

Stay in-character. Avoid inappropriate topics, OOC conflicts, or disruptive behavior that can break the immersion of others.

6. Follow Staff Instructions

All staff members have authority to manage the server. Disobeying instructions may result in disciplinary action.

7. No Advertising

Advertising other servers, services, or products without permission is strictly prohibited. Violators will be banned immediately.

8. Respect RP Boundaries

Avoid unrealistic or inappropriate roleplay scenarios. Always consider the safety, consent, and immersion of others.

9. No Spamming

Spamming messages, images, or voice chat is not allowed. Keep communication clear and concise.

10. Account Integrity

Using multiple accounts to bypass rules, restrictions, or bans is forbidden. Each member is responsible for their own account.

11. Keep Channels Organized

Post content in the correct channels. Avoid derailing conversations or creating unnecessary clutter.

12. Follow Roblox Terms of Service

All roleplay and interactions must adhere to Roblox's rules and guidelines. Breaking ToS may result in server action.

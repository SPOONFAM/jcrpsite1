Server Rules

Jackson County Roleplay enforces strict realism and professionalism. All members are expected to read, understand, and follow these rules at all times.

1. General Conduct

  • Respect all members and staff at all times.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • Follow all staff instructions without argument.
  • No exploiting bugs or game mechanics.

2. Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character at all times while in roleplay.
  • No random deathmatching (RDM).
  • No vehicle deathmatching (VDM).
  • Value your life in all situations.

3. Metagaming & Powergaming

  • No using out-of-character information in roleplay.
  • No unrealistic actions or abilities.
  • Roleplay injuries realistically.
  • No forcing roleplay outcomes.

4. Communication Rules

  • No OOC chat during active roleplay scenes.
  • Do not use voice changers without approval.
  • Radio usage must be realistic.
  • No mic spamming or soundboards.

5. Law Enforcement Rules

  • Follow department SOPs at all times.
  • No abuse of authority or equipment.
  • Pursuits must follow server pursuit policies.
  • Corruption requires admin approval.

6. Civilian Rules

  • Criminal roleplay must be realistic.
  • No constant crime without cooldowns.
  • Businesses must follow Civ Ops guidelines.
  • Mafia activity requires approval.

7. Vehicle & Pursuit Rules

  • No unrealistic vehicle usage.
  • FearRP must be followed during stops.
  • No ramming unless justified.
  • Air units require command approval.

8. Punishments

  • Warnings, kicks, and bans are at staff discretion.
  • Severe violations may result in permanent bans.
  • Ignorance of rules is not an excuse.
  • Appeals must be done through official channels.

Server Rules

Below are the in-game rules, banned items, and roleplay permissions for Jackson County Roleplay. Click each section to expand.

Random Death Match (RDM)

Randomly shooting or killing people is strictly prohibited. This also includes shooting at vehicles. Firearms should not be used out of roleplay.

Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Hitting or ramming other vehicles with the goal of desyncing them will result in moderation.

Respawning

Respawning during an active scene to avoid role-play will result in an instant kick.

Metagaming

Using information out of role-play to benefit your character is prohibited.

Realism

Keep actions realistic. Drifting excessively or using unrealistic avatars is prohibited.

Interference

Do not interfere with scenes that do not include you, including moderator scenes.

Tool Usage

Use tools for their intended purpose. Abuse or spamming of tools is prohibited.

Departments

Follow guidelines for your department as found in the department channel.

VC ONLY

You must be in a voice channel while in-game. No voice changers allowed.

Age Requirement

Must be 13 or older and behave appropriately in VC.

DUI Roleplays

DUI roleplays are allowed only in VC. In-game DUI discussion will result in a ban.

Priority

Priority is required for certain scenes. Request via !mod in-game. T4 & Boosters have automatic priority.

Permissioned Roleplays & Special Items

Booster/Contributor vehicles: 2020 Strugatti Ettore, 2016 Surrey 650S, 2017 Averon R8, etc.

Banned guns/cars: Model 29, M249, Remingtons, Pea Car.

Banned roleplays: Terrorist, Bomb, any breaking Roblox ToS.

Permissioned roleplays: Hostage (HR), Border (HR), Roadwork (Admin).

Full detailed rules are also available in the Discord server.

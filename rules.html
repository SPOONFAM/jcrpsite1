Server Rules

Click each rule to expand and see the full details for Jackson County Roleplay.

Random Death Match (RDM)

Randomly shooting or killing players is strictly prohibited. Firearms should only be used in roleplay scenarios.

Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Hitting or ramming vehicles out of roleplay to cause desync or griefing is not allowed.

Respawning

Respawning during an active scene to avoid roleplay consequences will result in immediate moderation action.

Metagaming

Using knowledge from outside roleplay for in-game advantages is prohibited.

Realism

Actions must remain realistic. Avoid excessive drifting, unrealistic avatars, or out-of-character behavior.

Interference

Do not interfere with scenes where your character is not involved, including moderator-managed events.

Tool Usage

Tools must be used as intended. Abusing or spamming tools is strictly forbidden.

Departments

Follow all department-specific guidelines as listed in the department channels.

VC ONLY

Players must be in a voice channel while roleplaying in-game. Voice changers are prohibited.

Age Requirement

Players must be 13+ and maintain appropriate behavior in voice channels.

DUI Roleplays

DUI scenarios are allowed only in voice channels. Discussing DUI roleplay outside VC is prohibited.

Priority

Priority is required for certain scenes. Use !mod in-game. Tier 4 & Boosters receive automatic priority.

Permissioned Roleplays & Special Items

Booster/Contributor vehicles: 2020 Strugatti Ettore, 2016 Surrey 650S, 2017 Averon R8, etc.

Banned guns/cars: Model 29, M249, Remingtons, Pea Car.

Banned roleplays: Terrorist, Bomb, or breaking Roblox ToS.

Permissioned roleplays: Hostage (HR), Border (HR), Roadwork (Admin).

Full detailed rules are also available in the Discord server.

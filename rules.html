Server Rules

Below are the in-game rules, banned items, and roleplay permissions for Jackson County Roleplay. Click each section to expand.

Random Death Match (RDM)

Randomly shooting or killing people is strictly prohibited. Firearms should only be used in roleplay scenarios.

Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Hitting or ramming other vehicles intentionally is prohibited.

Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in roleplay is strictly forbidden.

Realism

Actions must remain realistic. Drifting, unrealistic avatars, and overpowered roleplay is not allowed.

Departments

Follow department-specific guidelines in the designated channels.

Age Requirement

Players must be 13+ and behave responsibly in voice chat.

Permissioned Roleplays & Items

Booster vehicles: Strugatti Ettore, Surrey 650S, Averon R8.

Banned guns/cars: Model 29, M249, Remingtons, Pea Car.

Banned roleplays: Terrorist, Bomb, any Roblox ToS violations.

Permissioned roleplays: Hostage (HR), Border (HR), Roadwork (Admin).

Full rules available in the Discord server.
